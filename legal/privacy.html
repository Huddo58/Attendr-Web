Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Overview

Attendr is designed to operate entirely within your organisation’s infrastructure. We respect the privacy of organisations and their users and minimise the collection of personal information wherever possible.

2. Information Collected

The Attendr website may collect limited information such as:

  • Basic analytics information about site usage
  • Email addresses provided through contact or support requests
  • Licensing information required to generate valid software licenses

3. Software Data Handling

The Attendr application itself runs entirely inside your organisation's infrastructure when deployed on-prem. Staff presence data, user accounts, and operational information remain inside your own systems and are not transmitted to Attendr servers.

4. Licensing Information

When purchasing a license, limited information may be stored including organisation name, email address, and license identifiers. This information is used only for licensing validation and customer support.

5. Third-Party Services

Attendr may use trusted third-party providers for services such as payment processing or website analytics. These providers operate under their own privacy policies.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information collected through the Attendr website or licensing systems.

7. Updates

This privacy policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes to the product or applicable legal requirements.
